Privacy
Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

When you use the contact form on this site, we collect the information you choose to provide: your name, email address, subject, and message. We do not collect any other personal information, and we do not use cookies or analytics scripts to track visitors.

How We Use Your Information

The details you submit through the contact form are used for a single purpose: to respond to your inquiry. When you submit the form, its contents are sent as an email — via our email delivery provider, Resend — to the studio's inbox, with your email address set as the reply-to address so we can get back to you directly.

Data Storage & Retention

This site does not use a database. We do not store, log, or retain copies of the information you submit beyond the email generated by your submission. The only persistent record of your message is the email itself, held in our inbox and in Resend's delivery records in accordance with Resend's privacy policy.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing or any other purpose. The only third party involved in handling your data is Resend, which acts solely as the email delivery service for messages you choose to send us.

Your Rights

Since the only record of your submission is the email you asked us to send, you can request that we delete that email from our inbox at any time by contacting us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes to the site or applicable regulations. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out via the contact form or email us directly at hello@luminarystudio.com.
